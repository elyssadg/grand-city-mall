About Grand City Mall

Grand City Surabaya is one of the largest shopping center in the city, located in the heart of Surabaya, and easily accessible by public transportation. The mall's construction, with a 4.5 hectares area and a total of eight floors, was completed in March 2009. This shopping center is close to the Grand City Convention and Exhibition Hall. Mall guests who want to go to the exhibition area through each floor of the mall can use a 30-meter-long link road.

Grand City Mall Surabaya is an all-in-one shopping stop that are hosting all the favourite brands under one roof. The mall has more than 500 shops and restaurants, as well as a cinema, ice skating rink and bowling alley. There’s a number of well-known fashion and lifestyle brands for all ages in Grand City Surabaya for instance Pull & Bear, Levi’s, Mothercare, Nike, Adidas, Ace Hardware, Cotton On, H&M, Uniqlo, Victoria’s Secret, Zara, and many more. Grand City Surabaya is not only a heaven for the fashionista but also a paradise for those who love to eat. With a total of more than two hundred F&B tenants, customers will never run out of choices of place to eat. If you are looking for entertainment, Grand City Mall Surabaya also has a cinema, ice skating rink, and a bowling alley.

Grand City Mall hosts many events throughout the year, such as fashion shows, live music performances and cultural festivals. There are also special sales and promotions that often take place at the mall. So make sure to visit Grand City Mall when you come to Surabaya!
